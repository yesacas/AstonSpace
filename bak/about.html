About Our World

Aston first formed in 2013 as an informal gathering of individuals inspired by a deep learning of cultures. Initially these meetings centered out of a passion for cooking but later branched into other special areas including language, travel, culture and art.

As a growing metropolis with well established world class businessess and universities, as well as proximity to many popular tourist sites, our headquarters in Chongqing provides an increasing connection between East and West. With that connection came a growing diversity and an appetite to share this diversity. Aston satisfied and brought this diversity together under one roof, in one space, Aston Space.

And Aston Space keeps growing, relocating in the summer of 2014 from a small one-room space in Chongqing to our current spacious home near Guanyinqiao, Chongqing's meeting place. In the near future, we will be expanded the concept in San Francisco and Los Angeless. But for now, it is here in Chongqing where Aston truly has found its home, offering both residents and visitors a space to link to the world.
